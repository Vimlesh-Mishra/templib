If you are a PhD Student: 5 books, PG student: 4 books, UG Student: 2 books at a time for seven days.

Students can borrow books for a period of 07 days. Borrowed books can be renewed twice or more depending on availability of books. Your renewal request will not be accepted if the book is heavily overdue or in demand by others.

You can issue books for home lending from the Issue Section, but Reference and Rare Books are not issued.

To find a particular book you can use the OPAC on Intranet. You can check live status by clicking on details of the searched entry. http://10.1.4.38/autolibwebopac/ Else you can contact the Books Circulation Desk.

The DMCE College Library remains open between 8.45 a.m. to 7.00 p.m. on all working days. In the vacation period library timing changes to 9.00 am to 5.00 pm.

Nobody is allowed to borrow books from the Library on someone else’s card.

Any form of marking or writing in a book, whether it is with pencil or pen, is not permitted. Folding of corners of a book is also prohibited. Anyone vandalizing a book in this way is liable to be charged a fine to cover the cost of the book and also can be banned from using the Library.

Books are arranged on the shelf as per subject classified order (According to Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme). Guide charts are pasted on each rack for the exact location of books.

You should inform circulation staff immediately and replace the latest edition of the same book. If the book is out of print or not available in the market, then you have to follow the instruction of the Librarian.

If the damage is minor, you have to pay binding charges else you have to replace it with a new one.

For accessing the reading room you need to register, keep your bag at the baggage counter and maintain silence while using the reading room facility of the library.

Loss of the Library card should be immediately reported to the Library membership/ registration desk. The duplicate card shall be issued on adding a fine of Rs. 50/-.
