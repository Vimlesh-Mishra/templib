Vision

To provide access to information to its user in a technology-enabled environment and to become a significant learning resource center with updated Knowledge Resources in the Engineering domain.

Mission

M1: To provide access to high-quality information resources in all formats.

M2: To support the teaching, learning, and research mission of the institute.

M3: To provide library services with excellence and offer innovative library services.

Objectives

  • To establish and maintain an effective relationship with the students, faculty, researchers, and staff of the Institute to ensure an informed and integrated approach to the creation and delivery of information services.
  • To select, collect, organize, preserve, manage and provide access to information sources in print, non-print, and digital forms, regardless of physical location, to support scholarly pursuit, learning, teaching, and research in the Institute.
  • To facilitate and promote access to the fast-growing and expanding collection of digital sources of primary and secondary information.
  • To develop and provide quality collections/resources, user facilities, and library staff, to maintain an environment conducive to study and research.
  • To manage the information resources effectively and actively promote optimum usage.
  • To serve as a Learning Resource Centre for knowledge management development in the area of Engineering.
  • To update knowledge resources as per the requirement of users.
  • To serve as a community Resource Centre.

Library Special Features

  • Implementation of Barcode technology in the library along with Borrowers cards to faculty and students.
  • Library Collection organisation through Dewey Decimal Classification scheme which is an internationally known and accepted scheme for library classification.
  • Open Access is provided to users. Users are allowed to search books directly to the bookshelf.
  • Orientation Course regarding Library use for new students enrolled during the induction Program.
  • Guidance with respect to technical and organizational matters relating to libraries is extended, when asked for.
  • Special Reading room facilities for Faculty Members.
  • Syllabus and Question bank provided digitally .
  • The library is automated and all records are maintained by using Auto Lib NG library software.
  • Library website is developed for giving access to library resources and services .
  • Promotion for open access knowledge resourcess through library website and library services .
