The academic library has been described as the “Heart” of the learning community, providing a place for students and faculty to do their search and advance their knowledge. The librarians and library staff provide numerous services to these users, addressing their needs, characteristics, and interests.

It is an important service provided by the circulation section. Lending of documents to users is done by the issue desk of the central Library. Students are eligible to issue two books for one week against their library borrower card. Books reissuing facility is available. Library full automation is done. Issue return, catalog searching is regulated with LMS.

The kind and nature of reference service to be provided would depend upon curriculum, research programmers, and methods of teaching and objectives of university education. The aim of this service is to assist the user in the most effective use of resources.

Personally, staff assists users to get their information resources. In the changed e contents are widely available on the internet. Using search tools and techniques give assistance to users for searching the right piece of information become more important.

The library is providing open access to its users. Users have free entry to the racks. Guide charts are provided to direct the user to the books. Subject-wise book arrangement and DDC Classification is done.

The library is fully automated. ‘Autolib Library Management Software’ is used for this purpose. Database of books and members is updated. Searching books facility is provided.

Saving the time of users and library staff, we need to use technology in day-to-day functions. Library’s so many functions are reputable in nature. Using software for this purpose, we can save the time of our users and staff.

Daily functions like book lending, renewing, book searching, generating reports can be completed with the help of LMS.

Users are provided with ten computers with internet connections. Users can view electronic resources, educational video; NPTEL (provides E-learning through online Web and Video courses various streams.).

Library has a subscription to `Science Direct` e-journals Database.

The Central Library is providing a study room facility for staff and students. An airy hall with a seating arrangement of 200 users at a time. Silence environment is maintained. Drinking water and other facilities are provided inside the reading hall.

The library has maintained an archive of old to revised question papers and syllabus for users.

Book Bank Scheme is available for SC/ST/DT/NT caste category students. Students can issue a half set of books of their required books for the whole semester.

Photocopy service is available. Users are allowed to take a photocopy of the required document under fair use.

Central Library is providing newspapers to update knowledge on a day-to-day basis.
