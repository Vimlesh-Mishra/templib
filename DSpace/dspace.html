http://localhost:8080/jspui/

DMCE Institutional Repository is a set of services that Central library of institute offers to members of this community for management and dissemination of digital material generated by institute and community member.It includes Staff Publication, Students Publication, Project Reports, Syllabus, Question Paper Bank, E courseware, Digital assets generated by community members.

The main objective for having Institutional Repository is to provide open access to institutional research output by self-archiving in an open access repository .
